Eggs Woodhouse

This meal comes from Archer

Ingredients

  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 1/8 stick of butter
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • lemon juice
  • 2 warm artichoke hearts
  • sliced ham
  • Black Truffle
  • Saffaron
  • parika
  • caviar

Directions

  1. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Cook spinach until bright green and wilted.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add flour and whisk until combined. Slowly whisk in the milk. Continue whisking until the bechamel sauce thickens.
  3. Season the spinach with salt and pepper. Then, add the bechamel sauce and give it a good mix.
  4. In the bottom of an immersion blender cup, place egg yolks and squeeze lemon on top. Blend together.
  5. Slowly drizzle in melted butter and continue to blend until sauce formed.
  6. Plate 2 artichoke hearts on a bed of creamed spinach.
    7. \
  7. To poach eggs: In a pot, heat water over medium-high heat until almost boiling. In a mesh sieve, swirl eggs before submerging them in the water. Keep the eggs moving and retrieve them after 3 ½ minutes. Place poached eggs on top of the artichokes.
  8. Liberally stack Ibérico ham on top of the poached eggs. Shave black truffle on top of the ham. Pour warm hollandaise over the entire dish.
  9. Top with a pinch of paprika and kashmiri saffron. Add a spoonful of caviar and enjoy.
